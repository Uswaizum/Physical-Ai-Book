# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This textbook covers the fundamental concepts, advanced techniques, and practical implementations in the field of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## About This Book

This textbook is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics, from basic principles to advanced implementations. It covers:

- Theoretical foundations of physical AI
- Design and control of humanoid robots
- Machine learning applications in robotics
- Simulation and real-world deployment
- Advanced topics in embodied AI

## Getting Started

This textbook is structured to guide you from fundamental concepts to advanced implementations in physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Navigation

Use the sidebar to navigate through the different chapters and sections of the textbook. Each chapter builds upon the previous ones, providing a coherent learning experience.

## Prerequisites

This textbook assumes familiarity with:
- Basic programming concepts
- Linear algebra and calculus
- Introduction to robotics concepts
- Machine learning fundamentals

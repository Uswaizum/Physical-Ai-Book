# Chapter 2: Robotics Design and Humanoid Systems

## Overview

This chapter covers the design principles and engineering aspects of humanoid robots, including mechanical design, actuation systems, and sensor integration.

## Topics Covered

- Humanoid Robot Architecture
- Kinematics and Dynamics
- Actuator Systems
- Sensor Integration
- Balance and Locomotion

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you will understand:
- Key design considerations for humanoid robots
- Kinematic chains and inverse kinematics
- Different types of actuators and their applications
- How to integrate various sensors for perception
- Principles of balance and walking gaits
